Steps

1 Open LibreOffice Writer.

2 Click File>>Wizards>>Letter

3 Choose the type of letter and page design.

4 Select the items to be printed.

5 Specify the sender and recipient information.

6 Fill in the information you would like in the footer

7 Specify last settings
